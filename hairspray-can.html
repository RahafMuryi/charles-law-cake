Boyle's Law

Hairspray Can 💨

When volume decreases, pressure increases — and when released, the high-pressure gas pushes hairspray out.

P₁V₁ = P₂V₂
V↓ = P↑  ·  V↑ = P↓
Step 1 — Compressed gas The gas is trapped in a small volume inside the sealed can. Because volume is small, pressure is very high. Boyle's Law: P₁V₁ = P₂V₂.
"When gas volume decreases, pressure increases because gas particles are squeezed into a smaller space."
Volume ↓ → Pressure ↑
Temperature stays constant
