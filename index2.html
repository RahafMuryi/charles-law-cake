Gas Law 04

Boyle's Law — Lungs & Breathing

Pressure and volume are inversely related when temperature stays constant.

P₁V₁ = P₂V₂
·
Volume ↑ → Pressure ↓
Volume ↓ → Pressure ↑
Step 1 — Before inhaling The lungs are at a resting volume. Pressure inside the lungs equals the pressure outside. Everything is balanced — no air is moving yet.
"When lung volume increases, pressure decreases because air particles have more space to move."
