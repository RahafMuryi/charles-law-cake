Charles' Law

Pop-Up Turkey Timer 🦃

When temperature increases, gas volume increases — pushing the timer indicator upward.

V₁/T₁ = V₂/T₂
T↑  =  V↑
Step 1 — Cold turkey The timer is still down because the gas inside is cool and occupies a small volume. No popping yet!
"When temperature increases, volume increases because gas particles move faster and take up more space."
Temperature ↑ → Volume ↑
Pressure stays constant
